Understanding Burstiness

Regular (B = -1)

Events occur at perfectly uniform intervals. Think of a metronome or scheduled train arrivals.

Random (B = 0)

Events follow a Poisson process. Inter-event intervals are exponentially distributed.

Bursty (B = +1)

Events cluster in bursts with long gaps. Common in social communications and neural spikes.
